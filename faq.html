Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

We're based in Antioch, IL and provide on-location services throughout Lake County, McHenry County, and the greater Chicagoland area. We regularly serve brides in Lindenhurst, Grayslake, Waukegan, Libertyville, Lake Zurich, Barrington, and the northern Chicago suburbs. We're also happy to travel for destination weddings — just ask!

Yes! We highly recommend a trial run 4–8 weeks before your wedding. This allows us to perfect your look, test products on your skin, and ensure you feel completely confident on your big day. Trials are included in our Full Bridal Experience package or can be added to any other package.

We recommend booking 6–12 months before your wedding date, especially during peak season (May through October). Popular dates fill up quickly, so reaching out early gives you the best chance of securing your preferred date.

We use a curated mix of high-end, professional-grade products selected for long-lasting wear and photo-ready results. Our kit includes brands known for their quality and skin-friendly formulas. We customize our product selection based on your skin type, concerns, and preferences, and we're always happy to accommodate allergies or sensitivities.

Our makeup is designed to last 12–16 hours or more. We use professional primers, setting sprays, and techniques that keep your look flawless from ceremony to last dance. Many of our brides report their makeup lasting through tears, hugs, outdoor summer heat, and dancing without needing any touch-ups!
