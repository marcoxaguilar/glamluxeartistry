About Us

The passion, people, and purpose behind Glam Luxe Artistry

Meet Your Artist

Hi, I'm Megan Orozco

Hello! I'm the owner and lead artist behind Glam Luxe Artistry, based in Antioch, Illinois. What started as a creative hobby in my teenage years has grown into a thriving bridal beauty business that I'm incredibly proud of today.


With over 10 years of experience in the beauty industry, my love for makeup — especially bridal — has only deepened. There's something truly magical about helping a bride feel her most confident, radiant self on one of the most meaningful days of her life. That moment when she sees herself in the mirror and lights up — that's what I live for.


As demand grew, I built a team of talented, like-minded artists who share my dedication to excellence, warmth, and attention to detail. Together, we now offer both makeup and hair services for weddings and special events throughout Lake County, the Chicago suburbs, and beyond — creating a seamless, luxury experience for every client.


Glam Luxe Artistry is more than just beauty — it's about connection, confidence, and celebrating you at your most unforgettable.
